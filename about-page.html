About Us

GreenCampus is a college-wide initiative focused on promoting sustainable living and reducing environmental impact within our student body, staff community, and local area. We aim to empower individuals to make environmentally responsible choices through education, inspiration, and behaviour change. Our mission is to create a greener, more sustainable campus by fostering awareness, engagement, and action among our community members. We strive to provide resources, support, and opportunities for students and staff to participate in sustainability initiatives, events, and projects that contribute to a healthier planet.
