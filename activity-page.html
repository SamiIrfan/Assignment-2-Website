Weekly Activites

Monday

  • Reduces Food Miles
  • Grow produce food at home
  • Reduce your carbon emissions
  • Reduce your energy
  • Reduce the amount of water your using

Tuesday

  • Set up AC
  • Clean HVAC filters
  • Use natural cleaning products
  • Take a 1-minute shorter shower
  • Start a windowsill herb garden outside

Wednesday

  • Shop at charity/thrift shops
  • Wear clothes multiple times, not once
  • Use a laundry bag
  • Buy pre-loved furniture
  • Plant pollinator-friendly flowers

Thursday

  • Switch to soap/shampoo bars
  • Use washable makeup pads
  • Walk, bike, or use public transports
  • Use a library for books/media
  • Host a clothing swap with friends

Friday

  • Bring reusable shopping bags
  • Avoid pre-packaged fruits/veggies
  • Freeze food nearing use-by date
  • Buy in bulk to reduce packaging
  • Reduce the amount of money your using
